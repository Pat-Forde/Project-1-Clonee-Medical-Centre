About Us

 Appointments


For existing patients appointments please call the clinic or fill out our contact form.


Unfortunately, we are currently NOT in a position to accept new patients.

 Location


We are conveniently located on Clonee Main Street.


Free car parking is available and is located under the arch beside Flames restaurant.

  Medical Cards


Please note that our clinic does NOT accept Medical Cards, GMS cards or European Health Care Cards.

  Garda


We are registered members of the An Garda Síochána Doctor of Choice Scheme.

  Blood Testing


We offer full check ups alongside testing as needed during consultations.


Blood testing available Monday, Wednesday and Thursday mornings between 9am and 11.45 and must be booked in advance.

  Covid 19


For the safety of our patients and staff we encourage all to sanitise their hands on arrival and wear a face mask.


If you are suffering from COVID-like symptoms please take an antigen test to rule out COVID before attending our Surgery.
